About the Conference

Born in 2016 as a long-form event with a shared-interest, meetup feel; we paused for a few years during the COVID pandemic. In 2025 for our sixth year, Data-Driven Wisconsin returns to the Milwaukee School of Engineering and Direct Supply's Innovation and Technology Center in downtown Milwaukee!

Venue

Diercks Hall

Registration, Speaker Presentations, Sponsor Expo, and all meals/beverages

1025 N. Milwaukee St.
Milwaukee, WI 53202

Direct Supply Innovation & Technology Center

Networking Reception & Dinner

1020 N. Broadway
Milwaukee, WI 53202

Registration Hours

Wednesday, August 13: 7:30am - 5:00pm

Please check in at the Registration Desk inside Diercks Hall to receive your badge.

Parking

Designated parking for the DDWI event will be available in State Street Lot A (501 E. State Street).

⚠️ Cars parked in other MSOE lots will be ticketed.

The Viets Field parking structure will be closed to attendees, and the Water Street Lot requires payment. Street parking is available for up to 5 hours.

All day parking is available at many parking structures in downtown Milwaukee.

Hotels

There are several hotels in the downtown Milwaukee area convenient to the conference venue. We recommend booking early as August is a popular time in the city.
